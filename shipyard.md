## West Coast at Cost
Jun 2025 - Aug 2025

In the summer of 2025, I completed a mechanical internship at a local shipyard. I worked on many hands-on tasks, such as installing throttle cables, reinstalling onboard displays and assisting with the installation of the anchor system.
